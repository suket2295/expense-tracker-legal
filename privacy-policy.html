Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Expense Tracker & Budget Planner. The app is designed as an offline-first personal finance tool that helps users track expenses, budgets, income, reports, and related financial records on their own device.

1. Information We Collect

By default, Expense Tracker & Budget Planner does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with us or with third parties.

The financial information you enter, such as transactions, budgets, income values, categories, currency preference, and report calculations, is stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores your ledger data locally on your device using the app's private storage and local database. This may include:

  • Expense and income entries
  • Budget limits
  • Currency preference
  • Monthly income settings
  • Reports and calculated summaries
  • Security preferences such as app lock or biometric settings, where enabled

3. Financial Data

Your financial data is sensitive. The app is designed so that financial records remain on your device by default. We do not operate a server that stores your transaction ledger, budgets, or income information.

4. AI and Online Processing

In version 1.0, AI Copilot and online AI financial features are disabled or marked as pending. The app does not send your financial ledger data to an AI service by default.

If a future version enables optional AI features, those features will require user consent and this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain what data may be processed, which service provider is used, and how users can control it.

5. Receipt Scanner and Camera

If receipt scanning is available in the app, it is intended to help users enter transaction details more quickly. If camera-based scanning is enabled in a future version, the app may request camera permission only for scanning receipts. Camera access will not be used for background monitoring.

6. Data Export

The app may allow you to export your ledger data as CSV or JSON. Exporting data is user-initiated. Once exported, you are responsible for storing and sharing the exported file safely.

7. Data Deletion

The app provides a Reset Ledger or delete/reset option that allows users to clear local financial records from the app. Deleting local data removes the ledger data stored by the app on the device.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal financial data with advertisers, marketing companies, or data brokers.

9. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on standard Android system services for functions such as opening legal links in a browser, biometric authentication, or local file sharing when you export data.

If future versions integrate optional third-party services, this policy will be updated accordingly.

10. Security

We use reasonable local security measures to protect user data, such as private app storage and available Android security features. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Users are responsible for keeping their device secure.

11. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
