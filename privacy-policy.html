Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application Melav - Expense Tracker & Budget Planner. The app is designed as an offline-first personal finance tool that helps users track expenses, budgets, income, reports, and related financial records on their own device.

1. Information We Collect

Melav does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with us or with third parties.

The financial information you enter, such as transactions, budgets, income values, categories, notes, currency preference, and report calculations, is stored locally on your device.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores your ledger data locally on your device using the app's private storage and local database. This may include:

  • Expense and income entries
  • Budget limits
  • Categories and notes
  • Currency preference
  • Monthly income settings
  • Reports and calculated summaries
  • Security preferences such as app lock or biometric settings, where enabled

3. Financial Data

Your financial data is sensitive. Melav is designed so that financial records remain on your device by default. We do not operate a server that stores your transaction ledger, budgets, income information, or notes.

4. No Online Processing

In version 1.0, Melav works offline and does not send your financial ledger data to cloud servers, AI services, analytics servers, advertising networks, remote databases, or external prediction services.

Any summaries, reports, budget warnings, charts, or insights shown in the app are generated locally from the information you enter.

5. Internet Access and Legal Links

In version 1.0, Melav does not require an internet connection for its core expense tracking features.

If you open legal links such as the Privacy Policy or Terms of Service, your browser or another app installed on your device may open those pages. Those apps are separate from Melav and are controlled by their own privacy policies.

6. Receipt Helper

In version 1.0, Melav may include local template-based receipt helper features to make manual entry faster. The app does not use camera access, photo scanning, or background camera monitoring in version 1.0.

7. Data Export

The app may allow you to export your ledger data as CSV or JSON. Exporting data is user-initiated. Once exported, you are responsible for storing, protecting, sharing, uploading, or transferring the exported file safely.

8. Data Deletion

The app provides a reset, wipe, or delete option that allows users to clear local financial records from the app. Deleting local data removes the ledger data stored by the app on the device.

Exported files that you created outside the app may need to be deleted separately from your device storage or any place where you shared or uploaded them.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal financial data with advertisers, marketing companies, data brokers, or third-party analytics services.

10. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on standard Android system services for functions such as opening legal links in a browser, biometric authentication, app lock, or local file handling when you export data.

Opening legal links may use your browser or other apps installed on your device. Those apps are controlled by their own privacy policies.

11. Security

Melav uses local security measures to help protect user data, such as app-private storage, an encrypted local database, app lock, biometric authentication where enabled, and available Android security features.

Biometric authentication is handled by the Android system. Melav does not collect or store your fingerprint, face data, or other biometric identifiers.

However, no method of storage is completely secure. Users are responsible for keeping their device, screen lock, backups, and exported files secure.

12. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
